Growth of Privacy Tools
The last few decades have been a crucial time period for consumer privacy. As the internet and technology expanded our ability to connect with others, so did it expand the ability for third parties to breach our privacy. Recent events have helped bring to light the privacy explatations committed by companies and government entities. However, these events only matter if they affect consumer behavior, which is the key question here.

Has the use and interest in consumer privacy tools changed in relation to key historical events?

Google Trends measures relative interest in certain terms using the worldwide number of searches for those terms. A score of 100 corresponds to the highest interest level that any of these terms achieved over the time period. A score of 50 means half of that interest level.
